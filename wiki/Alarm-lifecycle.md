# Alarm lifecycle

Trigger ON stores `triggerAt = now + delaySeconds`. Trigger OFF keeps the schedule unchanged. Reset ON clears the schedule, repetitions, sensor state, and counters.

## Francais

Trigger ON stocke `triggerAt = maintenant + delaySeconds`. Trigger OFF conserve la programmation. Reset ON efface la programmation, les repetitions, l'etat du detecteur et les compteurs.
