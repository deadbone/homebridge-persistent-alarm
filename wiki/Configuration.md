# Configuration

Configure the `PersistentAlarm` platform with an `alarms` array. Each alarm needs `id`, `name`, `delaySeconds`, `motionDurationSeconds`, and `repeatMode`.

## Francais

Configurez la plateforme `PersistentAlarm` avec un tableau `alarms`. Chaque alarme demande `id`, `name`, `delaySeconds`, `motionDurationSeconds` et `repeatMode`.
